# Iconography

OpenSnap iconography should feel native to macOS.

## SF Symbols

Use SF Symbols wherever possible. They align with macOS stroke, weight, and optical proportions.

## Stroke Width

Prefer regular or medium symbol weights. Avoid heavy symbols unless required for contrast at small sizes.

## Corner Radius

Custom icons should echo the logo's softened rounded geometry without becoming playful or inflated.

## Filled vs Outline

- Outline symbols are preferred for toolbars and secondary controls.
- Filled symbols may be used for selected states or high-emphasis actions.
- Do not mix filled and outline styles in the same control group unless state requires it.

## Symbol Philosophy

Icons should make actions faster to recognize, not decorate the interface. If an icon is unclear, pair it with text or use text alone.
